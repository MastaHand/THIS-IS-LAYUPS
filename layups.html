Lay-Ups

Learning how to lay up is also pretty important. It helps you improve at scoring points.
You can also imrpove yourself at doing lay ups while someone is defending you.
It does take time but practice is all everyone needs. Giving up is not a way you get better.
Laying up is taking 2 steps to th hoop with the ball in your hand. Even if you miss you can try
to get a rebound and try to score again.
Listed Below are steps to make a Lay Up:

  1. Dribble the ball
  2. Hold the ball and take two steps
  3. Use the hand you are comfortable to throw the ball near the hoop
  4. If miss, then grab the ball again and throw it to the hoop
